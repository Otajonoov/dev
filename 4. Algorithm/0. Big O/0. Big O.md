- **n** - algoritmga kiruvchi elementlar soni
## Qiyosiy Jadval

| Murakkablik | n=10 | n=100  | n=1000    | Tavsif              |
| ----------- | ---- | ------ | --------- | ------------------- |
| O(1)        | 1    | 1      | 1         | Doimiy vaqt         |
| O(log n)    | ~3   | ~7     | ~10       | Logarifmik          |
| O(n)        | 10   | 100    | 1000      | Chiziqli            |
| O(n log n)  | ~30  | ~700   | ~10,000   | Chiziqli-logarifmik |
| O(n²)       | 100  | 10,000 | 1,000,000 | Kvadratik           |
