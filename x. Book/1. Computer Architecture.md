# Computer Architecture: zero-to-hero kitoblar

Bu ro'yxat bit/gate darajasidan boshlab CPU, ISA, pipeline, cache, performance va expert computer architecture darajasigacha olib boradi. Avval inglizcha original kitoblar tanlandi, keyin ruscha tarjima/nashr holati tekshirildi.

## O'qish tartibi

1. **Code** bilan bit, logic gate va CPU intuition hosil qilish.
2. **Nand2Tetris** bilan kompyuterni noldan qurib ko'rish.
3. **Digital Design and Computer Architecture** va **Structured Computer Organization** bilan hardware/software qatlamlarini mustahkamlash.
4. **Patterson/Hennessy**, **CS:APP** va **Computer Architecture: A Quantitative Approach** bilan professional va expert darajaga chiqish.
