# Distributed Systems: zero-to-hero kitoblar

Bu yo'nalish System Design'dan chuqurroq: failure model, replication, consensus, logical clocks, distributed transactions, fault tolerance va distributed algorithms. Avval inglizcha original kitoblar tanlandi, keyin ruscha tarjima/nashr holati tekshirildi.

## O'qish tartibi

1. **Understanding Distributed Systems** bilan developer-friendly umumiy xarita olish.
2. **Designing Data-Intensive Applications** bilan reliability, scalability, replication, partitioning va consistency trade-offlarini tushunish.
3. **Designing Distributed Systems** bilan cloud-native distributed patterns ko'rish.
4. **Distributed Systems** va **Distributed Systems: Concepts and Design** bilan universitet darajasidagi fundamentga o'tish.
5. **Introduction to Reliable and Secure Distributed Programming** va **Distributed Algorithms** bilan consensus, broadcast, snapshots va formal model darajasiga chiqish.
